For optimal email deliverability, we highly recommend using a subdomain instead of your main domain, it will increase your deliverability by almost 5%.
for example: use mail.yourdomain.com instead of yourdomain.com

By adding a subdomain like mail.yourdomain.com you can send emails using:

  • sales@yourdomain.com,
  • support@yourdomain.com,
  • info@yourdomain.com,
  • anything@yourdomain.com
